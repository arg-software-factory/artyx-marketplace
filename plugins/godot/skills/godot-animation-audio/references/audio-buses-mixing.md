# Audio buses and mixing

Route AudioStreamPlayer output to named buses such as Master, Music, SFX, and UI. Apply global
volume/effects on buses and local positional/loudness behavior on players. Do not use dB conversion
as an arbitrary linear slider. Load long music streams with an import/compression choice appropriate
to platform memory and latency.
